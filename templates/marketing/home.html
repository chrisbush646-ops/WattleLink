The Pipeline

One clear process.
Every step signed off.

WattleLink gives your team a structured, repeatable path from raw literature to approved claim. Each stage requires deliberate sign-off from a named reviewer. Nothing moves forward by accident, and nothing falls through the cracks.

01
Search & Ingest

Boolean PubMed queries with AI-assisted query building bring relevant literature directly into the platform. Open-access PDFs are ingested automatically and paywalled content is uploaded manually. Everything lands in one searchable, organised library rather than a shared drive folder that only some people know about.

PubMed · AI Query · PDF Extraction
02
Quality Assessment

Cochrane GRADE and Risk of Bias scoring applied consistently to every paper. AI pre-populates the assessment form and a qualified reviewer confirms each field. The result is a reproducible, evidenced quality rating rather than a judgement call that lives in someone's head.

GRADE · RoB 2 · ROBINS-I · Human Confirm
03
Summaries

A structured findings table and executive paragraph for every paper, with each statement traceable to a specific page in the source PDF. Your MSL team gets clarity they can act on in field conversations without having to interpret raw clinical papers themselves.

Findings Table · Executive Summary · Page References
04
Core Claims

AI-drafted claims paired with a fidelity checklist and a mandatory fair balance statement. Each claim is individually approved, edited or rejected by a named reviewer. No claim progresses without explicit human sign-off, and the decision is permanently recorded against the person who made it.

AI Draft · Fidelity Checklist · Fair Balance · Named Reviewer

A Culture of Compliance

Compliance works best when it is built into the process itself, not left to individual memory.

WattleLink makes the compliant path the easy path. Governance is embedded in every workflow, so your team does not have to think about it separately. The right process becomes the default.

Human in the Loop

AI does the heavy lifting and your team makes every decision. Claims, summaries and assessments each require a named, accountable reviewer before they progress. The platform will not let you skip this step.

Immutable Audit Trail

Every approval, rejection and edit is permanently recorded with a timestamp and the name of the reviewer. The log cannot be altered or deleted, which means your evidence of compliance is always reconstruction-ready.

Role-Based Access

Medical Affairs, Commercial and Admin roles with strict, platform-enforced boundaries. Commercial users cannot see unapproved content. This is a technical control, not a policy that relies on people remembering to behave correctly.

Australian Data Residency

All data stored in AWS Sydney (ap-southeast-2) and never leaves the region without explicit configuration. Built for Australian pharmaceutical data sovereignty requirements from the ground up.
